Tips to keep your Heart Heathy

Eat healthy(NOT trans fats)

Read the labels on all foods. Trans fat appears on the ingredients list as partially hydrogenated oils. Look for 0 percent trans fat. Make it a point to avoid eating foods with trans fat

Get Active (Stay at a healthy weight)

Quit smoking & Limit alcohol

Smoking is one of the main causes of cardiovascular disease, and smokers are almost twice as likely to have a heart attack.Alcohol can affect your heart by causing high blood pressure, abnormal heart rhythms and damage to the heart muscle.

Manage stress & Get enough sleep

Make sleep a priority. Get 7 to 8 hours of sleep most nights. If you have sleep apnea, you should be treated as this condition is linked to heart disease and arrhythmias.
